Why People Come Back

THE YORU
DIFFERENCE

YORU was born out of frustration. Manila had great restaurants — but nothing for the people who worked late, played late, or just couldn't sleep. We opened so the city's night shift finally had somewhere to go.

The broth takes 72 hours. The noodles are flown in from Fukuoka twice a week. The playlist is always too loud. That's the point.
